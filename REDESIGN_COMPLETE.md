## 🎓 Color Psychology Explained

### Green (Primary)
- **Psychology**: Health, nature, growth, harmony
- **Use**: Perfect for organic/natural products
- **Effect**: Creates trust  and credibility

### Cream/Beige (Background)
- **Psychology**: Neutral, clean, sophisticated
- **Use**: Backgrounds and negative space
- **Effect**: Premium, high-end feel

### Terracotta (Accent)
- **Psychology**: Earthy, warm, traditional
- **Use**: Badges, highlights, CTAs
- **Effect**: Authentic, trustworthy

### Sage Green (Secondary)
- **Psychology**: Calming, natural, balanced
- **Use**: Hover states, subtle accents
- **Effect**: Soothing, organic
